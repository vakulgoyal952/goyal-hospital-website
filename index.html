Advanced Orthopedic Surgeries

Specializing in orthopedic surgeries with excellence — the best orthopedic hospital in Jaipur for knee replacement, hip replacement, and more.

Hip Replacement

Hip replacement replaces the hip joint with a prosthetic implant to treat osteoarthritis, hip fractures, and arthritis. The main objective is pain relief and improved hip function — allowing patients to stand straight and walk normally.

Knee Replacement

Knee replacement replaces diseased joint surfaces with metal and plastic components, relieving pain and disability for patients with rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, and severe knee deformity.

Shoulder Surgery

Repairs muscles, connective tissue, and damaged joints from traumatic or overuse injuries. Common procedures include rotator cuff repair, dislocated shoulder repair, and shoulder impingement correction.
