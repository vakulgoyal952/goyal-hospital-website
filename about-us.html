About Goyal Hospital

Rajasthan's Leading Orthopedic & Joint Replacement Hospital · Established 1990

Our Legacy

35 Years of Trusted Excellence

For more than three decades, Goyal Hospital has delivered ethical, reliable and advanced orthopedic treatment to patients across Rajasthan and India. With over 16 lakh satisfied patients and more than 60,000 successful surgeries, we continue to help people regain mobility and live pain-free lives.

Established in 1990 in Shastri Nagar, Jaipur, Goyal Hospital has grown into the best orthopedic hospital in Rajasthan — renowned for joint replacement, ACL surgery, sports injury treatment, and comprehensive fracture care.
